Legal notice & privacy

How we handle your data on virderm.com. No unnecessary legalese.

Controller

The data controller is Virderm. You can reach us anytime at hola@virderm.com.

What data we process

This site is informational and does not collect data via automated forms. Only if you voluntarily write to hola@virderm.com do we process the data you choose to include in your message (at least your email address). We do not collect photographs or biometric data.

Purpose

To reply to your message and, if you ask, to let you know when Virderm becomes available in your country. We do not send third-party advertising or sell your data.

Legal basis

Your consent, given when you initiate contact with us and request information. You can withdraw it at any time.

How long

We keep your email until you ask us to delete it or the project no longer has this purpose.

Processors

Our email provider (ARSYS, Spain) operates the hola@virderm.com mailbox. Our web hosting provider (Cloudflare) processes technical access data (IP, browser) needed to serve the site. Both are bound by their own GDPR safeguards and processing agreements. Cloudflare also provides the aggregated audience analytics described below.

Your rights

You can access, rectify, erase, object to, restrict or port your data, and withdraw consent, by writing to hola@virderm.com. You can also lodge a complaint with the data protection authority in your country (in Spain, the AEPD — www.aepd.es).

Cookies

This site uses only technical cookies necessary for its operation. We do not use analytics or advertising cookies. To understand in aggregate how the site is used (page views, approximate country, browser and performance metrics), we use Cloudflare Web Analytics: a cookieless tool that stores nothing on your device, does not track individual people and builds no profiles. The legal basis is our legitimate interest in measuring and improving the site (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR); being anonymous and aggregated, it does not require your prior consent.

Age

This site is intended for people of legal age.
