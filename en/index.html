Frequently asked

What people ask before signing up.

  • Is this a medical app? +

    No. Virderm guides your skincare routine; it doesn’t diagnose or treat diseases. If we detect something beyond cosmetic care, we recommend you see a dermatologist.

  • What happens to my photos? +

    Facial analysis runs on your own phone. We don’t upload the raw image to our servers unless you allow it, and the data is deleted after 30 days by default. You’re in charge.

  • When do you launch? +

    We’re in pre-launch. The private beta kicks off in 2026, invite-only and with limited slots. If you want to try it, write to hola@virderm.com and we’ll let you know as soon as we open.

  • How much does it cost? +

    Freemium. The analysis and base routine are free; advanced features will be on a paid plan. You buy products wherever you want: Virderm doesn’t sell cosmetics.

  • Why only men? +

    Because men’s skincare is poorly served: it’s either ignored or oversimplified. And male skin is different by nature — thicker, oilier. We’d rather solve one audience really well than be average for everyone. It’s focus, not exclusion.

  • Are you multi-brand or do you have your own products? +

    Multi-brand, and we’ll never have our own. The moment we manufactured our own products, our recommendation would stop being neutral. That’s the line we don’t cross.

  • Who validates the scientific base? +

    Each active is backed by clinical literature with references (DOIs) you can check inside the app. The base is reviewed by a dermatologist before launch.
